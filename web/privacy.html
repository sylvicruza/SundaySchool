FaithFoundation

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FaithFoundation, the digital edition of the Opened Heavens Chapel Bible Study Manual 2026, handles information when you use the app and related web experience.

Effective date: 10 April 2026

App name: FaithFoundation

Organisation: Opened Heavens Chapel

Information We Collect

FaithFoundation is designed to support Bible study, reflections, and prayer. The app does not require account registration or login.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • study progress, such as lessons marked as studied
  • bookmarked lessons
  • personal reflection notes you create in the app
  • prayer requests you enter in the app

This information is stored locally on your device using app storage.

How We Use Information

The information stored by the app is used to:

  • remember your study progress
  • save your reflections and prayer requests for your own continued use
  • improve your in-app experience and continuity across sessions

Data Sharing

FaithFoundation does not sell your personal data.

Based on the current version of the app, reflection notes and prayer requests are stored locally on the device and are not submitted to a remote church database or backend service by the app itself.

If you use copy or share-related features, the content you choose may be copied to your device clipboard for you to paste elsewhere.

Third-Party Services

The app currently uses Firebase Hosting for the web version.

The app may also load a remote image resource in one prayer screen design. When remote resources are loaded, standard technical information such as IP address or browser/device request metadata may be processed by the hosting provider serving that content.

Children's Privacy

FaithFoundation is a Bible study application intended for church and faith-based educational use. Parents, guardians, and ministry leaders should supervise younger users where appropriate.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the app and its hosted services secure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and users should avoid storing highly sensitive personal information in app notes or prayer fields.

Your Choices

You may delete locally stored app information by clearing the application data or uninstalling the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app improvements, legal requirements, or ministry needs. Any updated version will be posted at this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Opened Heavens Chapel through its official church communication channels.
